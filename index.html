About Me: A Fusion of Design & Code

Hello! I'm Arihant Jain, a 20-year-old Full Stack Developer and UI/UX Designer who loves building digital experiences that blend creativity with clean code.

I bridge the gap between visual design and robust backend logic, ensuring beautiful, functional, and fast applications from end to end.

My Workflow

1. Understand Requirements

I begin by discussing your goals, target audience, and expectations to build a crystal-clear project vision.

2. Wireframing & Design

Using tools like Figma, I create wireframes and UI/UX prototypes for the ideal user journey and layout.

3. Development

Frontend (HTML, CSS, JS, React) and backend (Node.js, MongoDB, APIs) development with responsive best practices.

4. Testing & Feedback

Testing on all major browsers/devices, gathering feedback, and making improvements for performance & UX.

5. Launch & Maintain

Deployment on Vercel/Netlify/Render, followed by performance monitoring, SEO improvements, and maintenance.
